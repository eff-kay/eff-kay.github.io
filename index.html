Projects

  • Impulse Log (beta)

    I searched for the perfect productivity tool in the darkness of the unknown, and I fought many a monsters for it, but returned hopeless. So I decided to build one myself, and hope was risen anew, for it is he who taketh upon himself the toughest demons, gets fair lady.

    In short, I created this to scratch my own itch. Its a time tracking web app, that gives you the summaries and other analytics of the amount of time you spent on each project. This is not targetting towards companies where they can track employees, its for people like me who have a lot of side projects and wand to feel good to have worked on some of them

  • Iqbal Demystified

    Think of it as rapgenius for one guy's poetry. This is the project I am most proud of, altough my contributions were very small. Its an android app, which contains all the poetry, with its english translation and interpretations Dr Allama Iqbal. The interpretations are also crowdsourced and verified, the quality of community that we have managed to integrate is a special one. I mean who would want to contribute to an esoteric poetry.

    Anyhow all the credit goes to Azeem Zaheer, he was the one who started the project and mainly led the project, I acted as a mere minion.
